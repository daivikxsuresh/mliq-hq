The concept

Event-space pricing has no public benchmark, and a fixed-cost venue loses money on every day it sits empty. MLiQ quotes each incoming lead by the opportunity cost of committing the date now — turning an unsold day from a quiet loss into a priced decision. Built on The Glasshouses' own Salesforce history of held and lost opportunities.
